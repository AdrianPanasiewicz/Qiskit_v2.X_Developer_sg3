Questions you may ask during first meeting:  

Introductions:  
* Introduce yourselves  
* Share a bit about your study / work / etc. and why you joined the Qiskit advocate program

Goals and objectives:  
* Why does each member want to earn the Qiskit v2.X Certification?  
* Apart from obtaining the Qiskit v2.X Certification, what other related goals do the group members have? E.g. create closer connections to other Qiskit advocates, learn from others.  

Getting to know your group members’ study habits:  
* How does each member learn most effectively? If unsure, what have you done in the past regarding learning that has worked for you?
* What keeps each group member motivated?  

Study group structure:  
* How frequently are you going to meet?
* What days and times are best for the group to meet?
* What content or types of discussions are you going to cover when meeting?
* What are your group members going to do between meetings, if anything?
* What are going to be the roles and responsibilities for each member during and between the meetings? Will these roles and responsibilities be rotated among the group or stay the same over the entire study period?
